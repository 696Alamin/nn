The Eras Tour is the ongoing sixth concert tour by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift, who described it as a journey through all of her musical "eras".[1] An homage to her albums, the Eras Tour is her most expansive tour yet, with 146 dates across five continents.[2] It is her second all-stadium tour after the 2018 Reputation Stadium Tour.
